General questions

What is Bookify?

Bookify is an online bookstore with a mission to financially support local, independent bookstores.

We believe that bookstores are essential to a healthy culture. They’re where authors can connect with readers, where we discover new writers, where children get hooked on the thrill of reading that can last a lifetime. They’re also anchors for our downtowns and communities.

As more and more people buy their books online, we wanted to create an easy, convenient way for you to get your books and support bookstores at the same time.

Is Bookify a physical bookstore?

Bookify.org itself is not a physical store and does not purchase, print, publish, or warehouse books. Bookshop.org acts as a purveyor of new books (distributed solely through Ingram) by linking online shoppers with their favorite local vendors in order to traffic book profits toward indie booksellers with their preservation in mind.

If you have a specific book store you would like to support, we recommend using our Bookstore Finder to find their affiliate/book store page on our website. If you order through the site using their specific portal, all proceeds will go straight to their store instead of the general pool that is split up evenly among all Bookify registered bookstores.

Since Bookify is not a physical bookstore, Bookify cannot feature in-store / curbside pickup options. If you are looking for in-store or curbside pickup options we suggest you get in touch with your local bookstore directly to inquire if they have those ordering options available.

Does Bookify share customer info with affiliates?

We do not share our customer data with third parties. If customer orders from a bookstore’s shop page, the bookstore will be able to see the customer information, as Bookify considers those customers to be the bookstore’s customers. The bookstores have access to their customers and order information for their own sales.

Customer information is not made available for any other affiliate or partner of Bookify.

Do you ship to Canada or internationally?

No, we currently are US only. We do hope to help support bookstores over the world in the future.

Does Bookify support bulk custom bulk orders?

We do offer bulk ordering options! Bulk order inquiries can be sent to info@bookify.org with the email heading "Bulk Order Inquiry".
